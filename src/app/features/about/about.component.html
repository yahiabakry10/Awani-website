Pottery Notes: What Makes Each Piece Alive

You may notice some small crackles before or after using your pottery piece. This is completely normal and happens with pottery products all around the world.

These crackles happen because the clay body and the glaze react differently to heat. After the glaze cools and becomes solid, it may shrink a little more than the clay underneath. To release this tension, fine crackle lines may appear on the surface.

You may also hear a light ticking sound before or after using the product. This is caused by sudden temperature changes, also known as thermal shock. It is normal and usually stops after using the piece 2-3 times.

These little details are part of the natural beauty and character of handmade pottery.
