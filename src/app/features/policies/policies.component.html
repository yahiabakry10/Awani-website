Our Policies

Refund & Exchange Policy

We want you to love your order. If you receive an item with a manufacturing defect, damage, breakage, or if you receive the wrong item, you can request an exchange or refund within 24 hours of receiving your order.

Please note that we do not offer exchange, refund, or product inspection upon delivery.

Care Instructions

To ensure the longevity of your handmade pottery, please keep in mind that each piece is lovingly handcrafted and truly one of a kind. Subtle variations are part of its natural beauty.

  • Most of our pieces are dishwasher and microwave safe unless otherwise specified.
  • Avoid sudden temperature changes to prevent thermal shock.
  • Items with gold accents or special glazes should be hand-washed.

Shipping & Delivery

Each piece is carefully packaged to ensure it arrives in perfect condition. Since our items are delicate and handmade, we take extra care in preparing your order for transit. Please ensure you inspect your items within 24 hours of delivery.
